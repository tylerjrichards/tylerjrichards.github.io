Tyler Richards

Hey! I am a student at the University of Florida and an aspiring data scientist. I craft useful models, target valuable customers, and hack on personal projects that empower your business, project, or startup.

Whether I'm building a real-time emotion interpreter for individuals with autism, algorithms to connect pro-bono lawyers with at-risk individuals, or software to help small campaigns compete, I'm always looking for a new project, a new adventure.

I also spend my time teaching Data Science concepts and lectures through the UF Data Science and Informatics Student Group and through The Carpentries, as well as creating my own content. I specialize in soccer and political analytics within R and python. See here for more info!
